About us

Alhera foundation debuted in july 2019.It started its journy with only four members.Their goal was to help poor people of Bangladesh.These four were secondary level students of Qawmi madrasha.Basically ,the cries of many poor peole are floating in front of their eyes.As a result ,they formed the Al-Hera Foundation with the attiude to help the poor people.
